IETLS WRITING 2013 11 12

  • English

Nowadays the way many people interact with each other has changed because of technology.

In what ways has technology affected the types of relationships people make? Has this become a positive or negative development?

Recently, people are more and more liking to interact with others through modern technology, for example, the message, email, or call each othter with mobile phone. It’s undeniable that this makes our life more convenient than before, but there are also some problem accompany with the conenience injecting into our life. The most obvious one is that people direct interaction was drop down gradually.

Technology has changed our ways to communicate with others. It’s true that this bring us many benefits. In the ancient years, people can find the people who he want to interact(if the distance is not very large), or via the mail which sent by the postman. If you exanchange message with the mails, it’s always a long time for you waiting for the return message. Nowadays, we can’t bear this. The technology changed our interacting ways, and thoroughly. The simple example, if we want to contact others, we can make a telephone call, and you can make a connnection immediately disregarding the distance between you. If we want to write a mail to others, just write an email, and when we finished it and sent it, it will arrived to the recipient’s email box. As the development of the smart phone, such as iPhone or Android, we can share with others our position, so that somebody who may be interested you can communicate with you, or leave a private message, etc. All of these are not only fast but also funny. Technology has changed our ways of interacting with others so dramaticlly, but it is not to say there is no shortcoming brought to us.

There is a fact that we are so dependent on the modern technology, as a result, the technology has broaden our relationship with other people, such as our friends, our relatives, etc. at the same time when it brings us the benefit. we prefer call somebody to pay a visit to him, even though you are not very far from him. Thus, the chance for people gathering together has decresed sharply, so it has to be admitted the technology aliened us and our friends or relatives. There is also a increasingly severe social problem happend in our lives. As we can meet strangers on the net or throught the smart phone, at the beginning, this experience has some magic attractions for us, which is very stimulate or has some other magic. However, there are many people who were cheated by their net friends, even more, some beautiful ladies has been raped. So we should take more consideration when met the strangers.

Above all, technology enable us interact others more conveniently, and nobody hate that, nobody want to return back to the era that waiting for a long time just for a mail, (maybe there is some odds who enjoy the waiting feeling). However, we should spend more time with the people who are close to you, and we should reserve ourselves when we associate with strangers. In a word, we should be the master of the technology rather than the slave.
