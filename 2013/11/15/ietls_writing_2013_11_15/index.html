The diagram below shows changes in the popularity of cinema and television(1957-1974). Write a report on this topic.

This line Graph shows a trend that how popular of television and cinema are in the period from 1957 to 1974.

These two forms has differend trends. As we can see from the graph, the television has increasing trend whereas the cinema has a decreasing one. From 1957 to 1966, the television increased sharply from about 380 million to 750 million, and in the last 8 years, the figure rose steadily, reaching out to about 930 million until 1974.

There is a obivious contrast for the cinema comparing to tv license. It decread sharply from 1957. In the first 3 years, there is a dramatic fall from 940 million to 500 million. From then on, the rate of decline slowed down, the figure for 1966 is about 250 million. From this year on, there was a more gradual reduction in the popularity of the cinema reaching a figure of 90 million in 1974.
